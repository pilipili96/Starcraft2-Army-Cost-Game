Game Instructions:

You are presented with a random set of StarCraft 2 units. Your goal is to calculate the total cost of all the units combined. Keep in mind that minerals and gas have the same value.

Example:




In this example we see pictures of Probe, Battlecruiser, Zergling, Tank, and Roach. The correct answer is 1150. This is how it is calculated:

Probe = 50 total cost (50 minerals)

Battlecruiser = 700 total cost (400 minerals + 300 gas)

Zergling = 25 total cost (25 minerals)

Tank = 275 total cost (150 minerals+125 gas)

Roach = 100 total cost (75 minerals+25 gas)

So the total cost = 50+ 700+ 25+ 275+ 100=1150
